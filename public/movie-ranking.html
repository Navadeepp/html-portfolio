The Best Animes According To Me

My top 3 animes of all time

Death Note

"Death Note" offers a gripping narrative filled with intense psychological battles between its main characters, Light Yagami and L.

The classroom of elites

"The Classroom of the Elite" offers a compelling blend of psychological drama, social commentary, and strategic intrigue set within the confines of a prestigious high school.

Dr.stone

"Dr. Stone" presents a post-apocalyptic world where humanity has been petrified for thousands of years. The protagonist, Senku Ishigami, awakens to find himself in this stone age and endeavors to rebuild civilization using science and technology. The premise is fresh and offers a fascinating exploration of human ingenuity and innovation.
